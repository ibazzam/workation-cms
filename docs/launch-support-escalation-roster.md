# Launch Support Escalation Roster

This roster defines the active escalation chain for launch rehearsal and launch week operations.

## Effective Window
- Start: 2026-03-11
- End: until superseded by updated roster

## Escalation Chain
- Tier 1: Support Specialist On-Call
  - Responsibility: initial customer intake, case triage, SLA timer start
  - Channel: support desk queue + support incident channel
- Tier 2: Support Lead On-Call
  - Responsibility: approval routing for compensation bands, complex case resolution
  - Channel: support lead escalation alias
- Tier 3: Operations Lead / Incident Commander
  - Responsibility: cross-team coordination for service-impact incidents
  - Channel: incident bridge + operations escalation alias
- Tier 4: Rollback Owner + Executive Escalation
  - Responsibility: rollback approval and public-impact decisioning
  - Channel: launch command bridge
